About

Two decades on the line — now engineered with AI.

Info-Tech-Services pairs Toyota-style operational excellence with AI-enhanced technology — turning plant-floor discipline into measurable, production-grade outcomes for mid-market and enterprise manufacturers.

Who we are

A consulting firm that carries the weight of its own method.

We've spent 20+ years on plant floors across automotive, aerospace, oil & gas, heavy industry, paper & pulp, electronics, energy, and consumer products. We don't deliver slide decks — we deliver reliability programs, MES rollouts, AI models that earn their keep, and OEE gains you can point to on the control chart.

Every engagement combines two disciplines in one team: Lean and Toyota Production System practitioners who've led kaizens on the line, and engineers who ship AI-enhanced software into production. Shop-floor to boardroom — same team, same accountability.
