Philosophy

Three principles that drive every stack decision.

01

Production-grade from day one

No notebook handoffs. If a tool can't survive a full shift unattended, it doesn't ship.

02

Fit the problem, not the resume

We pick tools because they fit the outcome — not because they're trendy or because we already know them.

03

Open standards win ties

When two tools are equivalent, the one with open protocols and portable data wins.
