Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-13

Infotech Inc. (doing business as Info-Tech-Services) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit info-tech-services.com or contact us, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide: name, work email, company, role, and message content you submit through our contact form.
  • Automatically collected: IP address, browser type, referring URL, pages visited, and timestamps — collected via standard web analytics.
  • Engagement data: for active clients, any data shared with us under an SOW, governed by that SOW and any signed NDA.

2. How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and proposals.
  • To deliver services under signed engagements.
  • To improve our website and service offerings.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use contact-form submissions for unrelated marketing.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following categories of third-party services: web hosting (GitHub Pages), analytics (privacy-respecting), and form processing. Each is contractually bound to protect the information they process on our behalf.

4. Data Security

We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information we process, including encrypted transport (HTTPS), access controls, and secrets management. No internet transmission is 100% secure, but we work to industry-recognized standards (aligned with SOC 2 practices).

5. Cookies

We use a minimal set of functional cookies necessary to operate the site. We do not use advertising trackers. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without significantly impacting site usability.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (CCPA).
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email info@info-tech-services.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

Privacy questions: info@info-tech-services.com · Las Vegas, NV
