Predictive Maintenance Models

Failure prediction on rotating equipment, hydraulics, presses, CNCs, and heavy-industrial assets. Tuned against real failure history — not simulated data.

  • Alert routing to maintenance planners
  • MTBF / MTTR tracking

Computer-Vision Quality Inspection

Defect detection for surface, dimensional, and assembly faults at line speed. Deployed at the edge with cloud-side retraining loops.
